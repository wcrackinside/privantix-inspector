# Roadmap

The full product roadmap is maintained in [`docs/ROADMAP.md`](docs/ROADMAP.md).

High-level themes:

- **Data inspection** — `privantix-inspector` (tabular profiling, governance rules, exports).
- **ACL audit** — `privantix-acl-audit` (permissions, trusted groups, compliance signals).
- **Catalog & compare** — `privantix-catalog`, `privantix-compare`.
- **Ecosystem** — documentation hub [`privantix-docs`](https://github.com/wcrackinside/privantix-docs), public site [`privantix-web`](https://github.com/wcrackinside/privantix-web).
