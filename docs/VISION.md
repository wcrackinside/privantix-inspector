# Vision

## Product Vision

Privantix builds lightweight, portable tools that help organizations understand, inspect and govern their data repositories without requiring complex infrastructure.

The long-term vision is to reduce the operational gap between organizations with mature data governance programs and those that still depend on fragmented repositories, undocumented files and manual inspection processes.

Privantix Source Inspector is the first step in that vision.

It begins with repository inspection, technical metadata extraction and dataset profiling, and progressively evolves toward:

- data exposure mapping
- access and permission analysis
- risk scoring
- governance automation
- catalog integration
- continuous monitoring

## Problem Statement

Many organizations do not know:

- what data they have
- where it is stored
- how it is structured
- who can access it
- what level of exposure exists

This creates operational, security and governance risks.

Existing market tools are often too expensive, too complex or too infrastructure-heavy for smaller organizations or teams starting their governance journey.

Privantix addresses this gap through portable, practical and modular tools.

## Strategic Direction

Privantix will evolve through three layers:

### 1. Repository Intelligence
Inspection, profiling and technical visibility of files and datasets.

### 2. Exposure Awareness
ACL analysis, trusted group validation, access mapping and risk scoring.

### 3. Governance Automation
Rules, policies, scoring models, catalog integration and monitoring workflows.

## Principles

The product line should remain aligned with these principles:

- Portable by default
- Useful without complex deployment
- Focused on real repository problems
- Safe for governance and audit use cases
- Extensible through modular tools
- Friendly to both technical and semi-technical users

## Long-Term Outcome

Privantix aims to become a practical governance toolkit that helps organizations move from repository uncertainty to operational data visibility and control.
